Philippine Missile Project 1.0

Advancing Strategic Defense Research

The Philippine Missile Project 1.0 is an academic and conceptual initiative focused on understanding missile systems, defense technologies, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and embedded systems within a research-based framework.

Project Overview

This initiative explores theoretical and technological foundations of missile systems, including propulsion concepts, guidance mechanisms, AI-driven targeting systems, cybersecurity safeguards, and database management architectures. The project remains strictly educational and research-oriented.

Core Research Areas

• Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning Integration
• Embedded Systems Programming
• Cybersecurity & Defense Infrastructure Protection
• Database & Information Management Systems
• Legal and Compliance Frameworks
